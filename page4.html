EXOPLANETS

Exoplanets are planets that orbit stars outside our solar system. They come in many sizes and types, and studying them helps scientists understand how planets form and whether life might exist beyond Earth.
Some exoplanets may have conditions that allow liquid water, making them possible places for life. Scientists study these worlds to see if life could exist beyond Earth.
