SPACE TRAVEL

Today, astronauts travel to space using powerful rockets, most often to reach the International Space Station (ISS), where they live and work for weeks or months conducting experiments.
Private companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin, and others are making space travel more accessible, developing rockets and spacecraft that could one day carry tourists, scientists, and even settlers beyond Earth.
Robotic spacecraft and satellites explore planets, moons, asteroids, and distant parts of the solar system, sending back valuable data that helps scientists understand space without risking human lives.
