WORMHOLES

A wormhole is a theoretical passage through space-time that could link two distant parts of the universe. It’s often imagined as a shortcut, letting something travel across vast distances instantly.
Wormholes come from solutions to Einstein’s equations of general relativity, but none have ever been observed in real life. They remain purely hypothetical and a topic of research.
If a wormhole could be stabilized, it might allow fast travel across the universe or even connect different dimensions. However, keeping one open would require exotic matter with unusual properties.
